Hello World!

I am a Data Science Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Washington working in William Stafford Noble's lab. I received my PhD in Bioengineering from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and my bachelors degree in Computer Science from the Math Department at Reed College, a small liberal-arts college in Portland, Oregon. Follow the links above to learn more about me.

At this point in time, my main academic interests are machine learning for computational biology. I am especially interested in the application of machine learning for multimodal biological data. I am also interested in machine learning for protein science and engineering. I sometiems also dabble in applying machine learning to non-biological complex systems.
